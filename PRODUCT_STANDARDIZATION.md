## NAMING CONVENTIONS

### Do's ✓
- Use Title Case for all names
- Keep names concise (2-4 words max)
- Use descriptive adjectives (Premium, Luxury, Classic, Modern)
- Be specific about features (Stone-Embellished, Hand-Embroidered)
- Use professional fashion terminology

### Don'ts ✗
- Avoid brand names or location-specific terms in main categories
- Don't use abbreviations (Borka, Abaya are exceptions)
- Avoid vague terms like "Nice" or "Beautiful"
- Don't mix languages
- Avoid overly complex descriptions
