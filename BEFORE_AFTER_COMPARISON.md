# Borka Bazar - Before & After Standardization

## 📊 Categories Transformation

### BEFORE (Inconsistent)
```
1. Dubai Borka
2. Jumeirah Borka
3. Karchupi Borka
4. Nida Fabric Borka
5. Abaya Collection
6. Printed Borka
7. Party/Wedding Borka
8. Custom Design Borka
```

**Issues:**
- ❌ Location-based names (Dubai, Jumeirah)
- ❌ Fabric names in category (Nida Fabric)
- ❌ Inconsistent naming patterns
- ❌ Unclear descriptions
- ❌ Not professional for international audience

---

### AFTER (Professional & Standardized)
```
1. Classic Borka
2. Premium Borka
3. Artisan Borka
4. Summer Collection
5. Modern Abaya
6. Printed Collection
7. Occasion Wear
8. Custom Design
```

**Improvements:**
- ✅ Descriptive, not location-based
- ✅ Consistent naming pattern
- ✅ Professional terminology
- ✅ Clear descriptions for each
- ✅ International e-commerce standard
- ✅ Better for SEO and search
